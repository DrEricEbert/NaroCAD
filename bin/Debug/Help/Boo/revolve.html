Revolve - draws the shape resulted by revolving one shape around another shape

Syntax:

void Revolve(shape1: integer; shape2: integer; double: angle)

Arguments:

shape1: index of the node representing the shape that will be revolved

shape2: index of the node representing the shape that shape1 will be revolved around

angle: angle of revolution in degrees


Usage sample:

s1=Sketch(0,0,0,2,3,0,4,5,0) p1=Point(s1, 2,2,0) p2=Point(s1,2,5,0) l1=Line(p1,p2) p3=Point(s1,6,2,0) p4=Point(s1,6,5,0) l2=Line(p3,p4) Revolve(l1,l2,360)
